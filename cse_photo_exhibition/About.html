EXMO 2017

PHOTOGRAPHY EXHIBITION
APRIL 28TH & 29TH, 2017
13' & 14' BATCHES

Photos taken by students of CSE13 and CSE14 batches for DE2550 – Digital Photography module are exhibited in this photography exhibition. They were taken at the University premises, Galle Fort and Negombo.

Much of the work we see these days lacks the emotional impact to draw a reaction from viewers, or remain in their hearts. Though these students study Engineering streams, they have an artistic eye and taste towards artistic observation. There are many skilled and talented photographers among them as well.

“Photography is an art of observation,
little to do with the things you see
but
everything to do with the way you see them”
