Department of Mechanical Engineering

Department of Mechanical Engineering of University of Moratuwa is the leading department on Mechanical Engineering in Sri Lanka. The department is dedicated to researches and innovations in various fields including automotive engineering, energy engineering, product design and manufacturing engineering and mechatronics and automation.

The mission of the Department of Mechanical Engineering of University of Moratuwa is to produce engineers and researchers with sound knowledge on fundamentals of traditional, modern and emerging areas of engineering together with innovative design abilities, IT and managerial skills, which are essential to achieve sustainable national development.
